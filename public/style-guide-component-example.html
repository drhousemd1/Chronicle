Resistance Scoring System
  • ALIGNED: +10 points
  • SOFT_RESISTANCE: -5 points
  • HARD_RESISTANCE: -10 points

  • Rigid threshold: -50 → deviated
  • Normal threshold: -30 → failed
  • Flexible threshold: -20 → failed
Clone-on-Recovery
  • When a fail-branch recovery step succeeds, the failed success step is cloned as a new pending retry step
  • Rigid: Unlimited retries
  • Normal: Max 4 retries
  • Flexible: Max 2 retries

  • Max retries badge: text-red-500/80 bg-red-500/10 border-red-500/20
